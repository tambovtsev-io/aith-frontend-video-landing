## The Brief

Aurora Skincare approached us after seeing our documentary work for a Scandinavian travel brand. They wanted something that felt editorial — more *Kinfolk* than *Cosmopolitan*. The brief was deceptively simple: **show where our ingredients come from and why it matters.**

## Our Approach

We divided the shoot into two chapters:

1. **Origin** — Wide-angle landscape work in Iceland and Sweden, capturing the raw environments where Aurora's key botanicals grow wild. We shot during the golden hour over multiple days to maintain a consistent, warm palette.
2. **Ritual** — Intimate studio and lifestyle sequences filmed in Stockholm, focusing on texture, application, and the everyday ritual of skincare.

The edit weaves the two chapters together so the viewer constantly moves between vast nature and personal close-ups, reinforcing the brand's connection between land and self-care.

## Technical Details

- **Camera:** ARRI Alexa Mini LF paired with Cooke S7/i primes
- **Lighting:** Predominantly natural light, supplemented with Astera Titan Tubes for the studio sequences
- **Grade:** A desaturated, filmic grade with lifted shadows and warm mid-tones
- **Delivery:** 4K DCI master, 16:9 and 9:16 social cuts, 15-second pre-roll ads

## Results

The brand film launched alongside Aurora's Q2 product line and drove a **42% increase** in website sessions during launch week. It went on to win a Bronze at the Berlin Commercial Awards.

> "Luminary understood our ethos from the first call. The final film feels exactly like our brand — quiet, confident, and deeply rooted in nature." — *Emma Lindqvist, CMO, Aurora Skincare*
