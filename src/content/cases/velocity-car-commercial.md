## The Brief

Velocity Motors was preparing the launch of their first fully electric sports sedan — the **VM-1**. They had a legacy of combustion performance, and the brief was clear: *make electric feel fast, visceral, and uncompromising.*

## Our Approach

We crafted a narrative around **silence and speed**. The film opens in near-silence — a desert road at dawn, heat shimmer on asphalt — before the VM-1 erupts into frame. The contrast between the quiet electric powertrain and the raw velocity creates an emotional tension that carries the whole piece.

### Key creative decisions:

- **No voiceover.** The car speaks for itself. A minimal, pulsing electronic score builds throughout.
- **Practical effects.** Dust plumes, brake glow, and tyre smoke are all real — no CGI augmentation.
- **Single-take hero shot.** A 22-second unbroken tracking shot from the Russian Arm that follows the VM-1 through a canyon switchback.

## Technical Details

- **Camera:** RED V-RAPTOR XL with Canon CN-E Flex Zoom
- **Stabilization:** Russian Arm mounted on a Porsche Cayenne chase vehicle
- **Drone:** DJI Inspire 3 with full-frame Zenmuse X9 for aerial overheads
- **Frame rate:** Key action sequences shot at 120fps for dramatic slow-motion
- **Grade:** High-contrast, crushed blacks, electric-blue accent tones matching the VM-1 brand palette

## Results

The spot debuted during prime-time broadcast and generated over **18 million views** in its first week across YouTube and social platforms. Velocity Motors reported that pre-orders for the VM-1 tripled compared to their previous model launch.
